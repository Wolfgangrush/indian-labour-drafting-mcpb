# State Exemplar — Maharashtra

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** Maharashtra Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act 2017
- **Rules:** Maharashtra Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules 2018
- **Applicability threshold:** 10 or more workers
- **Registration:** Section 6 of the 2017 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 26 (₹1 lakh — first offence; up to ₹2 lakh — subsequent / continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Facilitators appointed under Section 25 of the 2017 Act

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** Maharashtra Labour Welfare Fund Act 1953
- **Rules:** Maharashtra Labour Welfare Fund Rules 1953
- **Contribution:** Biannual (June and December) — employee ₹6 + employer ₹18 + Government ₹6 = ₹30 per employee per period (verify current rate before applying)

## Profession Tax
- **Act:** Maharashtra State Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act 1975
- **Slabs:** Notified per the latest State Budget; salary slab-based deduction by employer

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (THREE-TIER — Maharashtra-specific)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters
- **Industrial Court (under MRTU & PULP Act 1971):** Maharashtra-only forum for (a) recognition of trade unions, (b) unfair labour practice complaints under Schedules II, III, IV of MRTU & PULP Act 1971

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** Industrial Disputes (Bombay) Rules 1957 (as adapted for Maharashtra)

## MRTU & PULP Act 1971 (Maharashtra-only)
- **Forum:** Industrial Court (separate from Labour Court / Industrial Tribunal)
- **Unfair labour practices:** Schedules II / III / IV (employer side / union side / mixed)
- **Recognition of trade unions:** Application before the Industrial Court for recognition certificate
- **Pendency:** Complaint under MRTU & PULP is independently maintainable; not affected by parallel ID Act proceedings
