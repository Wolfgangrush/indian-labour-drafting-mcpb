# State Exemplar — Gujarat

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** Gujarat Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act 2019
- **Rules:** Gujarat Shops and Establishments Rules 2019
- **Applicability threshold:** 10 or more workers
- **Registration:** Section 6 of the 2019 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 28 (fine up to ₹1 lakh; additional fines for continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Facilitator under Section 25 of the 2019 Act

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** Gujarat Labour Welfare Fund Act 1953
- **Rules:** Gujarat Labour Welfare Fund Rules 1953
- **Contribution:** Biannual — employee ₹6 + employer ₹12 + Government ₹6 (verify current rate)

## Profession Tax
- **Act:** Gujarat State Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act 1976
- **Slabs:** Notified per the latest State Budget

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (TWO-TIER)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** Gujarat Industrial Disputes Rules 1966
