# State Exemplar — West Bengal

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** West Bengal Shops and Establishments Act 1963
- **Rules:** West Bengal Shops and Establishments Rules 1964
- **Applicability:** All shops and commercial establishments in notified areas
- **Registration:** Section 16 of the 1963 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 25 (fine up to ₹250; additional fines for continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Inspector under Section 19

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** West Bengal Labour Welfare Fund Act 1974
- **Rules:** West Bengal Labour Welfare Fund Rules
- **Contribution:** Annual — employee ₹3 + employer ₹15 + Government ₹3 (verify current rate)

## Profession Tax
- **Act:** West Bengal State Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act 1979
- **Slabs:** Notified per the latest State Budget

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (TWO-TIER)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters
- **West Bengal-specific:** Industrial disputes have a long-running tradition; multiple Labour Court benches across the State

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** West Bengal Industrial Disputes Rules 1958
