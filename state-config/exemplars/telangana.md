# State Exemplar — Telangana

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** Telangana Shops and Establishments Act 1988 (formerly Andhra Pradesh Shops and Establishments Act 1988, applicable to Telangana post bifurcation)
- **Rules:** Telangana Shops and Establishments Rules 1990
- **Applicability:** All shops and commercial establishments
- **Registration:** Section 3 of the 1988 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 42 (fine up to ₹500; additional fines for continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Inspector under Section 35

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** Telangana Labour Welfare Fund Act 1987 (formerly AP Labour Welfare Fund Act 1987)
- **Rules:** Telangana Labour Welfare Fund Rules 1988
- **Contribution:** Annual — employee ₹2 + employer ₹5 + Government ₹2 (verify current rate)

## Profession Tax
- **Act:** Telangana Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act 1987
- **Slabs:** Notified per the latest State Budget

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (TWO-TIER)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** Telangana Industrial Disputes Rules (adapted from AP Industrial Disputes Rules 1958 post bifurcation)

## Four Labour Codes 2019-2020 — Notification Status (as of 2026)
- Verify against latest Telangana gazette before pleading

## Common labour-forum locations in Telangana
- Labour Courts at Hyderabad, Warangal, Karimnagar, Khammam
- Industrial Tribunal at Hyderabad
