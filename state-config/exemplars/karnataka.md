# State Exemplar — Karnataka

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments Act 1961
- **Rules:** Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments Rules 1963
- **Applicability:** All shops and commercial establishments in notified areas (most urban areas of Karnataka)
- **Registration:** Section 4 of the 1961 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 30 (fine up to ₹500; additional fines for continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Inspector / Senior Labour Inspector / Assistant Labour Commissioner

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund Act 1965
- **Rules:** Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund Rules 1968
- **Contribution:** Annual — employee ₹20 + employer ₹40 + Government ₹20 = ₹80 per employee (verify current rate)

## Profession Tax
- **Act:** Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act 1976
- **Slabs:** Notified per the latest State Budget

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (TWO-TIER)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters
- No equivalent of Maharashtra's Industrial Court for unfair labour practices — UFP claims, if any, route through standard ID Act framework

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** Karnataka Industrial Disputes Rules 1957 (as adapted)
