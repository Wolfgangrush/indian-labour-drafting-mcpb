# State Exemplar — Uttar Pradesh

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** Uttar Pradesh Dookan Aur Vanijya Adhishthan Adhiniyam 1962
- **Rules:** Uttar Pradesh Dookan Aur Vanijya Adhishthan Niyamavali 1963
- **Applicability:** All shops and commercial establishments in notified areas
- **Registration:** Section 3 of the 1962 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 22 (fine up to ₹500; additional fines for continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Inspector under Section 19

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** UP Labour Welfare Fund Act 1965 / cognate provisions under State-specific framework

## Profession Tax
- **Status:** UP does NOT levy Profession Tax under State Act

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (TWO-TIER)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters
- **UP Industrial Disputes Act 1947** — separate State Act with parallel jurisdiction; the Drafter checks whether Central or State Act applies based on the industry / employer category (the State Act applies to State Government undertakings and to industries notified by the State Government)

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** UP Industrial Disputes Rules 1957

## UP-specific overlays
- **UP Industrial Disputes Act 1947** — independent State Act; covers State undertakings and notified industries
- **UP Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act 1946** — adapted with State amendments
