# State Exemplar — Delhi (NCT)

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** Delhi Shops and Establishments Act 1954
- **Rules:** Delhi Shops and Establishments Rules 1954
- **Applicability:** All shops and establishments in the NCT of Delhi
- **Registration:** Section 5 of the 1954 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 40 (fine up to ₹500; additional fines for continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Inspector / Senior Labour Inspector under Section 36

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** Delhi Labour Welfare Fund Act has limited application — primarily through Central Labour Welfare Fund framework

## Profession Tax
- **Status:** Delhi does NOT levy Profession Tax

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (TWO-TIER)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters — Labour Court at Karkardooma
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** Delhi Industrial Disputes Rules 1958

## Forum locations
- Labour Court / Industrial Tribunal: Karkardooma Court Complex
- EPF Appellate Tribunal: Located in Delhi for matters from northern and central States
- POSH Internal Committees: Constituted at each Establishment under Section 4 POSH Act 2013
