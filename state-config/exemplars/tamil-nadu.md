# State Exemplar — Tamil Nadu

## Shops and Establishments
- **Act:** Tamil Nadu Shops and Establishments Act 1947
- **Rules:** Tamil Nadu Shops and Establishments Rules 1948
- **Applicability:** All shops and commercial establishments
- **Registration:** Section 5 of the 1947 Act
- **Penalty for contraventions:** Section 41 (fine up to ₹500; additional fines for continuing contraventions)
- **Inspector:** Inspector under Section 38

## Labour Welfare Fund
- **Act:** Tamil Nadu Labour Welfare Fund Act 1972
- **Rules:** Tamil Nadu Labour Welfare Fund Rules 1973
- **Contribution:** Annual — employee ₹20 + employer ₹40 + Government ₹20 (verify current rate)

## Profession Tax
- **Act:** Tamil Nadu Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act 1992
- **Slabs:** Notified per the latest State Budget

## Industrial Court Hierarchy (TWO-TIER)
- **Labour Court (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule II matters
- **Industrial Tribunal (under ID Act 1947):** Schedule III matters

## Tamil Nadu-specific labour overlays
- **Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (Conferment of Permanent Status to Workmen) Act 1981** — workman employed for 480 days within 24 months becomes permanent — workman-classification overlay
- **Tamil Nadu Catering Establishments Act 1958** — separate registration for hotels, restaurants, etc.

## Industrial Disputes Rules
- **Rules:** Tamil Nadu Industrial Disputes Rules 1958
